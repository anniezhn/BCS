Kurtan Lab at University of Rochester

Kurumada-Tanenhaus (KurTan) Lab is run by members of MTan Lab and Rochester Kinder Lab. Our goal is to understand language comprehension and production with special attention to how language users process multiple sources of information online. Comprehenders need to detect and analyze multiple levels of linguistic representations (e.g., sounds, words, grammar and discourse) while integrating information about the context, the speaker, and the content of the utterance so that they can plan and produce their responses in a timely manner.
